Sirb

Sirb is a geometric design that looks like a flock of birds. I created it in 2023. It consists of 6 identical "birds" arranged in a circle. Each bird consists of 3 identical rhombuses. Each rhombus has an interior angle of 30° and a side length of 1. "Sirb" means "flock" in Arabic.

Observations

  • Sirb has 6-fold rotational symmetry and an area of 9.
  • Sirb encloses a polygon that has 18 sides, a perimeter of 18, and an area of 4.53+6≈13.8
  • The smallest circle that encloses Sirb has a radius of (36+52) 4 ≈3.6
