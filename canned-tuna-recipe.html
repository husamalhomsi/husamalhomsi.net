Canned Tuna Recipe

This recipe takes less than 5 minutes and serves 1 person.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (104 g) of solid light tuna in oil
  • 2 loaves (76 g) of white pita bread
  • 3 tsp of yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp of hot sauce
  • 3 tsp of dried chopped onion
  • 1 tsp of dried chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Open the can, drain the oil, then empty the tuna onto a plate.
  2. Add the mustard, sauce, and onion to the tuna, then mix them slightly.
  3. Sprinkle the parsley over the tuna.
  4. Serve with the bread on the side.
