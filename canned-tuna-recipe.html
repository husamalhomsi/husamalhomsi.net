Canned Tuna Recipe

This recipe takes about 5 minutes and serves 1 person.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (104 g) solid light tuna in oil
  • 2 loaves (76 g) white pita bread
  • 3 tsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 3 tsp onion flakes
  • 1 tsp parsley flakes

Instructions

  1. Open the can, drain the oil, then put the tuna on a plate.
  2. Add the mustard, sauce, and onion, then mix.
  3. Sprinkle the parsley over the mix.
  4. Serve with the bread on the side.
